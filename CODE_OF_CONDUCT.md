# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project free from harassment and welcoming regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity or expression, experience level, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, sexual identity or orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive and specific.
- Accept responsibility and learn from mistakes.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.
- Protect users by keeping sensitive wallet and personal data private.
- Respect intellectual-property and open-source license obligations.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, doxxing, publication of another person's secrets or personal information, deliberate security abuse and sustained disruption are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns through a private repository-owner channel. Do not disclose sensitive evidence publicly. Maintainers may edit or remove content, reject contributions, temporarily restrict participation or permanently ban participants when necessary to protect the community.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant 2.1.
